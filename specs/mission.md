# Mission

Garlic Sauce is a presentation app purpose-built for online trainers.

## Problem

Online trainers fight their tools every day. They juggle screen-sharing
settings, alt-tab between apps to draw, and pray their students can't see
their private notes. The standard workflow — PowerPoint + screen share +
a separate whiteboard — creates friction that pulls focus away from teaching.

## Vision

A single, self-contained desktop application where trainers can:

- **Import** their existing PowerPoint slides without losing formatting.
- **Present** slides in a dedicated window that is safe to screen-share.
- **Read notes** in a separate, private window only the trainer can see.
- **Draw & annotate** directly on slides with mouse-friendly tools.
- **Switch to a whiteboard** canvas without leaving the app.
- **Edit notes** with rich-text formatting during or between lessons.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Trainer-first** — every feature is evaluated from the perspective of
   someone teaching a live online class.
2. **Self-contained** — no dependency on PowerPoint, LibreOffice, or any
   external application at runtime.
3. **Screen-share-friendly** — the slide window is always safe to share;
   private content lives in separate windows.
4. **Mouse-friendly** — drawing and annotation tools must be comfortable
   to use with a mouse, not just a graphics tablet.
5. **Small, shippable phases** — the product is built incrementally; each
   phase delivers usable value.
